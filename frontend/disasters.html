Disaster Types

Descriptions, causes, indicators, prediction probability, and safety tips.
Simulate Prediction
Earthquake
Probability: 45%

Sudden ground shaking caused by tectonic plate movement or fault rupture.

Causes
Tectonic shifts, volcanic activity, induced seismicity.
Risk indicators
Foreshocks, elevated seismic readings, plate boundary stress.
Safety tips
Drop, cover, and hold on. Secure heavy items. Prepare an emergency kit.
Flood
Probability: 72%

Overflow of water submerging normally dry land, often driven by rainfall and river discharge.

Causes
Heavy rainfall, storm surge, dam failure, poor drainage.
Risk indicators
River level spikes, saturated soil, persistent rainfall, warnings upstream.
Safety tips
Move to higher ground. Avoid flooded roads. Turn off power if instructed.
Hurricane
Probability: 55%

Powerful rotating storm system with high winds, heavy rain, and storm surge impacts.

Causes
Warm ocean waters, low wind shear, organized convection.
Risk indicators
Low pressure core, strengthening winds, satellite storm structure, sea temperature.
Safety tips
Follow evacuation orders. Secure windows. Stock water, food, and batteries.
Wildfire
Probability: 48%

Uncontrolled fire in vegetation areas influenced by fuel dryness, wind, and ignition sources.

Causes
Drought, lightning, human activity, powerline faults.
Risk indicators
High temperature, low humidity, strong winds, dry fuel indices.
Safety tips
Prepare defensible space. Evacuate early if advised. Wear masks for smoke.
Tsunami
Probability: 22%

Series of large ocean waves caused by undersea earthquakes, landslides, or volcanic activity.

Causes
Seafloor displacement, submarine landslides, volcanic eruptions.
Risk indicators
Strong coastal quakes, rapid sea withdrawal, siren warnings.
Safety tips
Move inland/uphill immediately. Do not return until all-clear.
Landslide
Probability: 40%

Down-slope movement of rock/soil triggered by rain saturation, earthquakes, or slope instability.

Causes
Heavy rain, seismic shaking, erosion, deforestation.
Risk indicators
Cracks in ground, leaning trees, blocked drainage, repeated rainfall.
Safety tips
Avoid steep slopes during storms. Evacuate if cracking/rumbling occurs.
