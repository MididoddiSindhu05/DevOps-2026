Evacuation tips
Before leaving
  • Pack go-bag: water, meds, ID copies, flashlight.
  • Turn off gas/electric if instructed.
  • Inform family of your route and meeting point.
During evacuation
  • Follow official routes; avoid flood waters.
  • Keep radio/phone charged for updates.
  • Assist children/elderly; stay together.
If sheltering
  • Stay away from windows and unstable walls.
  • Keep water purification and first aid ready.
  • Monitor alerts for “all-clear” only.
After event
  • Check for injuries and hazards (gas leaks).
  • Document damage for assistance.
  • Use text/SMS to reduce network load.
